# Design Direction

The IEEE AP-S PESU website will follow a hybrid design approach inspired by modern technology platforms and academic research labs.

## Hero Section — Inspired by Stripe

* Large bold title
* Minimal text
* Dark gradient background
* Clear call-to-action buttons
* Clean spacing and typography

Goal: instantly communicate identity and professionalism.

---

## Dark Theme — Inspired by Supabase

* Deep navy / dark gradient backgrounds
* Blue accent highlights
* Clean card components
* Minimal but modern interface

Goal: create a technical and modern feel suited for an engineering community.

---

## Projects Showcase — Inspired by MIT Media Lab

* Visual project cards
* Project pages with descriptions, images, and links
* Emphasis on research and technical work

Goal: highlight student research, innovation, and technical contributions.

---

## Content Structure — Inspired by IEEE AP-S

* Clear organization of sections
* Events, resources, and research categories
* Professional and academic presentation

Goal: maintain credibility and align with IEEE standards.

---

## Overall Design Principles
Clean
Minimal
Technical
Professional
Consistent
